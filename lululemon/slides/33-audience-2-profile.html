Lifestyle and values
Fitness is a proxy for professional discipline and peak performance mindset. Invests in quality gear as a commitment mechanism, not a luxury. Social fitness is a networking and identity tool. Appearance matters but is framed as performance optimisation, not vanity.
  • Cost-per-wear logic: a AUD 180 training short that lasts three years is a better investment than a AUD 60 alternative that pills in six months. Price sensitivity is the lowest of the three audiences.
  • Once adopted, loyalty is strong: switching cost is high when this man has a drawer of ABC pants that fit. Community context (run clubs, gym peers, office culture) provides sustained social reinforcement.
