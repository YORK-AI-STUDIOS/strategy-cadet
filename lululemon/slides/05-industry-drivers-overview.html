Why this matters now

Australian activewear has crossed a structural threshold. In 2025, 55% of Australians indicated a preference for athleisure and relaxed styles, and sports apparel now accounts for the largest single segment of AU activewear retail at approximately AUD 2.3 billion.

Online spend in Australia reached AUD 82.6 billion in 2025, up 14% year-on-year, with fashion and apparel commanding 21.38% of e-commerce share. Brands with strong DTC digital infrastructure are capturing margin and data that wholesale cannot provide.

The category has stopped being niche and started being dominant. That shift rewards brands with genuine technical differentiation, community infrastructure, and pricing confidence.
