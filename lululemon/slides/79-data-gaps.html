Appendix · Data gaps & confidence

What this report cannot claim, and what would close each gap.

Disclosing the limits of the evidence is part of the method, not an apology for it. Each gap below names exactly what could not be verified, and the costed upgrade path that would close it. The methodology is built to be upgraded.

Core report · market and brand evidence
Trustpilot AU and Global fully blockedUncertain

Both lululemon.com.au and www.lululemon.com Trustpilot pages returned HTTP 403 on all fetch attempts. Trustpilot is one of the highest-traffic AU review destinations. Excluded from star average throughout this report.

To closeA browser-session scrape or licensed Trustpilot data feed would add the missing review channel.
Meta Ads Library blocked; sourced via third-party indexUncertain

Meta Ads Library (facebook.com/ads/library) returned HTTP 403 to the fetch agent. Ad data is sourced from GoMarble (2026-03-08, 250-ad sample) and AdLibrary.com. Counts reflect a sampled global inventory, not a direct AU-filtered pull.

To closeA browser-session scan with AU country filter applied directly to Meta Ads Library isolates AU-specific creative volume.
AU ad count is estimated, not confirmedProbable

Global Meta ad count is confirmed at 362 active ads. AU country filter was not independently applied. AU-specific count is PROBABLE to be a subset, likely lower, consistent with standard geo-targeting practice for international brands.

To closeDirect browser-session Meta Ads Library scan filtered to Australia resolves this in under 30 minutes.

Lorna Jane did not appear in any of the three public ad intelligence tools scanned (GoMarble, AdLibrary.com, Meta Ads Library). Absence is a data gap, not confirmed absence of Meta activity. TikTok-first strategy is separately confirmed.

Data extensions · method limits
Share of search is relative, not absoluteProbable

Google Trends reports relative interest, not search volume, and is sampled. The 3-month and 12-month windows use different internal rescaling -- cross-window comparisons are directionally valid but not arithmetically precise. 24-month window was not pulled in this run.

To closeA paid search-volume subscription converts relative interest to absolute query counts; 24-month window added on next run confirms structural trends.
GEO scan is a web-search proxyUncertain

AI-visibility data uses web search as a proxy for direct AI-model querying. True GEO tracking requires direct API access to ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. Results may lag actual model behaviour by weeks to months.

To closeDirect paid-API access to the major frontier AI models, queried on a fixed prompt panel, closes this gap.
ProductReview sample is small; structured review not available for all rivalsVerified

Lululemon brand n=146 and online store n=88 are sufficient for theme extraction. ProductReview structurally over-represents 1-star ratings. Rivals listed on ProductReview have thinner sample sizes. Ajust added 12 AU complaint cases.

StatusResolved within spec. A commissioned AU consumer survey (n=400+) would upgrade to statistically robust CSAT.
Reddit, SaySo, Thingtesting, Mozo blockedUncertain

Reddit direct fetch was blocked. SaySo.com.au, Thingtesting, and additional AU community platforms returned HTTP 403. AU community and forum conversation is a real gap. The r/lululemon (~700K members) signal is sourced via web synthesis only.

To closeA licensed social-listening platform (Brandwatch or Meltwater) captures forum and Reddit conversation at scale, including AU geo-filtering.
Social engagement rates not calculableUncertain

Platform scraping was blocked across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube. Follower counts are sourced from search-index metadata that lags by days. Engagement rate (likes, comments, saves per post) was not captured for any platform.

To closeA paid social-analytics API (Sprout Social, Brandwatch, Emplifi) returns verified engagement rates across every platform at scale.
Every gap above is documented inline in the report at its point of use. Confidence chips: Verified, primary plus corroboration; Probable, single source or inference; Uncertain, partial or proxy data.
