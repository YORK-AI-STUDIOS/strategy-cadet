What this means
Lululemon holds #3 structurally. The category signal is Nike shedding share to Adidas, not to Lululemon.

Lululemon's 12.2% is consistent across both time windows (3-month: 11.9%). The brand is neither growing nor losing in search terms. The strategic implication: hold is structural, not growing. Nike's -2.7pp slide is flowing entirely to Adidas, not to Lululemon's lane.

  • P.E Nation +100% relative growth in 3 months: the watch item for Lululemon's premium AU positioning.
  • Lululemon's October trough is the lowest-cost media window: counter-seasonal brand investment opportunity.
  • The Upside flat at 1.1%: not a current threat.
