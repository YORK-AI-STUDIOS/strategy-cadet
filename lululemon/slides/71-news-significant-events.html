Activity rhythm

Two campaign waves, an APAC community push, an aggressive AU retail build.

  • Q1 AU (Jan-Mar): Peak AU activation window. Australian Open (Tiafoe Jan 2025), Lewis Hamilton GP edit (Mar 2026), Spring collection, new store openings.
  • APAC spring (Sep-Nov): Campaign season. "Together We Grow" and "Be Well, Be Together" both ran here. Community events, ambassador-led programming.
  • Dec-Jan: Global brand disruption in FY2025/26. CEO departure, proxy fight launch, product quality crisis and earnings all landed in this window.
  • Mid-year: Relatively quieter on campaign activity. Retail store openings cluster here (Miranda, South Australia, Brookfield).
The through-line

AU expansion is the proof point the global story needs.

With the US business under margin pressure and governance in public contest, Australia's 9.1% athleisure market share and 34-store footprint is the healthiest part of the brand's international narrative. Any agency engagement should lead with AU momentum and insulate from the global noise.
