Private (AU-owned)
Verified
The brand

The heritage AU brand defending on community and TikTok, not on paid media spend.

Lorna Jane is the dominant AU-born activewear brand: nearly 100 stores nationwide and $166 million in revenue (2024). Its "Active Living" philosophy centres on women's performance-functional and fashion-led athleisure. It holds 1M+ Instagram followers, making it the only AU-native brand within reach of Lululemon's regional account (405K).

Lorna Jane's marketing is deliberately low-cost and TikTok-first, relying on owned social and influencer content. Its TikTok "always-on" model is documented at 95% CPA reduction and 4x ROAS. It appears in 7 of 8 AU GEO queries, more than any other competitor in this set.

Why it matters to Lululemon

Lorna Jane owns the "Australian activewear heritage" and "yoga AU" GEO framing that Lululemon cannot claim by origin. Its TikTok moat is the clearest channel threat in the set: any AU activewear brand ceding TikTok to Lorna Jane risks losing the 18-34 female cohort. Lululemon's AU TikTok presence is unconfirmed.
