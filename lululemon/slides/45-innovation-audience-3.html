Gen Z active explorers, aged 18 to 27. The strongest seven ideas built around community co-creation, social commerce, and identity expression.

Audience 3 · Ten Types applied
Probable
Brand

Created by Us: Community Co-Design Drops

AU followers vote on colourway, print, or silhouette decisions for a limited capsule. Voting mechanics are public, the outcome is transparent, and the resulting drop is framed as a community artefact rather than a brand product. Gen Z buys things they feel they made.

Now3 to 6 months
Brand

Lululemon x AU Creator Collective

Identify 8 to 12 micro-creators in Australia’s fitness, wellness, dance, and outdoor adventure niches with 10,000 to 80,000 highly engaged followers. Product access and event priority, with authentic creation. No scripted content. No brief.

Now3 to 6 months
Channel

TikTok Shop Integration for AU Drops

Activate TikTok Shop for the Australian market with direct in-app checkout on drop days. Pair with creator briefings 24 hours ahead of drop activation to seed demand before the shop link goes live. Gen Z does not leave TikTok to make impulse purchases; meet them where the decision happens.

Now3 to 6 months
Customer Engagement

Challenge Culture: Monthly Active Identity Challenges

Monthly community challenges with visible public tracking: a movement streak, a trail completion, a recovery habit. Challenges generate content and reward participation with exclusive access rather than discounts. Being seen by peers is a primary motivator for this cohort.

Now3 to 6 months
Product System

Capsule Companion: Matching Set Architecture

Every new launch offered as a co-ordinated two-piece (sports bra or bralette plus leggings, shorts plus crop) photographed as a set and purchasable as a set at a 10% bundle price. Content-friendly, identity-complete, and a higher-basket transaction.

Now3 to 6 months
Profit Model

Student Discount Programme

A verified student discount of 15% via Student Beans or UNIDAYS for AU university students. Removes the price barrier for the most aspirationally motivated segment and builds habituated brand loyalty before full earning power is established.

Now3 to 6 months
Service

Instant AI Styling: Chat-Based Outfit Build

A conversational AI styling assistant (text plus image input) on the AU website and app that builds complete outfit suggestions from a selfie, a mood description, or an activity input. Optimised for Gen Z’s browsing behaviour: fast, visual, low-friction.

Now3 to 6 months
Read across
All seven priority ideas are Now-horizon.

For this cohort, the entire priority set is executable within six months. The co-design drop, Creator Collective, and TikTok Shop are the acquisition engine. The matching-set architecture and AI styling tool lower the barrier to first purchase. The student discount and challenge culture build habitual engagement before income grows to support full-price loyalty.
