What this means
Lululemon's IG engagement rate (1.72%) outperforms Nike (1.55%) - quality of community, not just scale.

The AU @lululemonausnz account (405K) is comparable to Lorna Jane (1M IG) at sub-half-scale - meaningful headroom. The TikTok gap (1.4M vs Adidas 11.2M, Nike 7M+) is the clearest unexploited asset given the brand's community-led identity.

  • Facebook engagement at 0.01% (vs 1.72% IG) = 170x gap. Likely poor spend ROI.
  • Threads (844K) ~= X (863K) but Threads is growing while X declines.
  • YouTube neglected: 306K subs, only 194 lifetime videos in 20 years.
