Part 05 · Competitive Context · Review themes

The themes split by channel, not by product. Every complaint traces to the online experience; every praise to the physical store or the legacy product.

Complaint themes3 themes · ProductReview + Ajust verbatims
1. Returns and exchange policy: rigid, ACL-adjacent tension60%+ · dominant
The single highest-volume complaint. Customers report being denied exchanges on sale items, items that don't fit, and items they believe are ACL-protected. Online-to-store inconsistency compounds the frustration.
“Store refused exchange on sale item with receipt and tags. Policy is rigid, outdated and incredibly unfriendly.”
Mary K. · ProductReview (Online Store) · 12mo ago · 1-star
▲Sentiment trend: steady to growing
2. Product quality decline: pilling, stitching, thinning fabric40-50%
Multi-platform signal: ProductReview AU (both listings), web editorial (Sept 2024), and r/lululemon globally. Lululemon's repairs programme explicitly excludes pilling - acknowledging the issue while declining to remediate it.
“Thin see through material.” / “Threads unraveled before first wear.”
Natasha + Emily · ProductReview (Online Store) · 2y ago · 1-star
▲Sentiment trend: growing - reaching editorial velocity
3. Customer service: dismissive staff, AI deflection, slow resolution35-40%
6-10 business day response times for AU team; bot-fronted support replacing human agents. Melbourne Factory Outlet specifically cited as "the worst" for unprofessional staff. In-store city flagships score materially better.
“Everything's AI. The lack of ownership over the problem was frustrating.”
Fiona C. + Eva · ProductReview / Ajust.com.au · 12mo ago / 2025 · 1-star
▶Sentiment trend: steady, emerging AI-deflection sub-theme
Praise themes3 themes · positive minority + web synthesis
1. Longevity and durability of legacy piecesDominant praise
Loyal customers citing 7-10+ years report older Lululemon pieces outlasting all competitors. Increasingly framed as historical praise rather than current-state endorsement - making it both praise and an implicit quality-decline signal.
“I have leggings and tops that are over 10 years old and still look as good as new.”
Unattributed · web synthesis · 2024
▶Sentiment trend: stable in volume, historicising in framing
2. In-store staff: knowledgeable, sizing-savvy, warmConsistent signal
City flagship stores (Melbourne Emporium, Sydney CBD) praised for educators who diagnose fit and function needs. Staff described as willing to assist with special orders and sizing advice. Directly contrasted against online service failures.
“Friendly, knowledgeable staff - willing to assist with sizing and special orders.”
Positive cohort · ProductReview (brand) · 2024
▶Sentiment trend: stable - clearest competitive strength in AU
3. Product design, functionality and aestheticCult loyalty
Specific product lines command strong loyalty: ABC pants, Scuba hoodie, Define jacket, Align leggings. Functional pockets, flattering cuts, performance fit, gym-to-street versatility. Design is not under threat; quality execution is the fracture point.
“Leggings, ABC pants, Scuba and Define pieces cited as holding up for years.”
Web synthesis · 2024
▶Sentiment trend: stable - design intent praised, execution questioned
