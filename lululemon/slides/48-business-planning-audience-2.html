Plan read

For this cohort the plan is built around earning respect before earning revenue. The Men’s Collective and SweatLife Hub establish performance credibility; the Fit Intelligence and Transparent Performance tools convert credibility into considered purchase. Loop and Lift is framed entirely in performance language, with no sustainability overlay. Channels run through elite sport, not consumer lifestyle media.
