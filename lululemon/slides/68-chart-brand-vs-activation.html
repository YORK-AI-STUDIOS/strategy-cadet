What this means
Meta works while brand equity is intact. If equity erodes - and the proxy fight is testing that hypothesis - the performance-only posture cannot self-repair.

Competing with Lululemon on Meta means competing against a high-volume retargeting machine. The attack vector is brand-building in channels Lululemon deprioritises: YouTube (9-10 uploads/year at 306K subs), AU-specific OOH, earned media with AU ambassadors.
