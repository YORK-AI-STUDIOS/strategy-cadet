Affluent male performance enthusiasts, aged 25 to 40. The strongest seven ideas built around performance credibility, community, and product excellence.

Audience 2 · Ten Types applied
Probable
Brand

Lululemon Lab for Men

A distinct men’s content platform within lululemon AU, anchored to elite male athlete performance stories, training science, and gear-engineering transparency. Separate content cadence and tone from the women’s channel. A distinct brand voice, not a gender-flipped copy.

Now3 to 6 months
Channel

Performance-First PDP

Redesign the AU men’s product detail page to lead with technical specifications: fabric weight, compression grade, seam type, UV rating, temperature range, and activity suitability. Move lifestyle photography to secondary position. This audience reads spec sheets before buying running shoes.

Now3 to 6 months
Customer Engagement

Men’s Performance Collective

Formalise men’s training community groups in each major AU city with weekly structured sessions: track, strength, trail, open water, led by certified performance coaches. Separate events from mixed-gender programming to give this audience a distinct social identity.

Now3 to 6 months
Service

Gear Performance Audit

In-gym gear assessment sessions where an educator evaluates a customer’s current training kit for performance gaps, fit issues, and wear-cycle timing, then provides a trade-in and upgrade recommendation. Transforms the trade-in from a chore into a performance optimisation service.

Now3 to 6 months
Product Performance

ABC 2.0: The 12-Hour Performance Trouser

The next generation ABC (Anti-Ball Crushing) Pant with Warpstreme 2.0 fabric: active moisture management, temperature regulation for Australian climate, and revised belt-loop geometry. Backed by a 90-day performance trial against comparable Vuori and Patagonia trousers with public data.

Now3 to 6 months
Product System

The Performance Stack

Explicitly merchandised performance stack systems for each training discipline: strength, run, yoga, recovery. Matching aesthetic, technical compatibility, and a named bundle discount. Reduces decision fatigue for a time-poor audience and drives multi-SKU transactions.

Now3 to 6 months
Customer Engagement

Strava and Garmin Integration

Partner with Strava and Garmin Connect to trigger lululemon milestone rewards at performance thresholds: first 10km, first half marathon, 30-day training streak. Converts performance data into brand touchpoints and positions lululemon as an active participant in the athlete journey.

Next6 to 12 months
Read across
Performance credibility runs through every idea.

For this cohort the idea bank demands that lululemon earn respect before it earns loyalty. The Lab for Men content platform, the performance-first PDP, and the Men’s Collective are the trust infrastructure. The ABC 2.0, Performance Stack, and gear audit convert that trust into transactions. Strava integration is the long-game engagement loop.
