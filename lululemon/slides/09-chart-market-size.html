What this means
Lululemon competes in the USD 8.12B AU activewear market, which is forecast to reach USD 10.91B by 2030.

The broader USD 412B global activewear scope includes casual and lifestyle wear and is not directly comparable. The AU figure is the working number for this brief; the GlobeNewswire/Research and Markets methodology aligns to Lululemon’s competitive set.

  • 5.11% CAGR is below global, but premium tier outpaces category average.
  • IBISWorld AUD 4.2B figure scopes to brick-and-mortar / direct fitness stores only.
  • Over 50% of yoga and Pilates participants sit in the top two socioeconomic quintiles.
