App surfaces personal sustainability metrics: kilograms of textile diverted, litres of water saved vs new production. This audience shares sustainability data socially. In-store trade-in counter; app sustainability dashboard; social share prompts; Certified Pre-Loved platform listing. Trade-in attach rateper new purchase transaction

Surfaces fabric recommendations, not just size: "Based on your history, you prefer less waistband compression." Profile is also accessible in-store for fitting appointments. App and website PDP integration; in-store educator access at fitting appointment; post-purchase email follow-up sequence. Online return ratereduction post-tool adoption

A gift concierge within the app curates men’s gift builds with educator-authored notes. Concept 05Transparent Performance SKU-level impact labels on every garment (QR-linked to material origin, certifications, end-of-life guidance) and an independent-audit annual progress report. For a cohort that cross-checks claims before spending $180 on leggings, verified transparency converts sceptics into advocates. In-store QR label infrastructure; brand.lululemon.com.au sustainability hub; editorial partnerships with Australian environmental journalism. Sustainability NPSamong loyalty members
Plan read

For this cohort the plan is a retention and advocacy engine, not a pure acquisition engine. Every concept reinforces quality, community, and transparency values that this audience already holds. The SweatLife Hub and Fit Intelligence tool are the stickiness mechanics; Loop and Lift and Transparent Performance are the values alignment; the gifting angle on Men’s Collective is the upside revenue unlock.
