Competitor 05

P.E Nation Sydney-founded fashion-first athleisure. The fastest-moving competitor in SoS, doubling from 1.5% to 3.0% in the last 3 months.

Private (AU-founded, est. 2016)
Verified
The brand

Under-indexed on media spend, over-indexed on editorial credibility and recent search momentum.

P.E Nation is a Sydney-founded activewear label (est. 2016, founders Pip Edwards and Claire Tregoning) positioned at the intersection of sport and street fashion. Known for bold colour-blocking and retro-inspired silhouettes, it is available at David Jones, THE ICONIC, and Stylerunner. Primary competitor in the fashion-first athleisure niche.

P.E Nation is regularly featured in Vogue AU, marie claire, and Russh, and holds strong GEO presence in "Australian gym clothes" and fashion-gym editorial content. It lacks the retail footprint, marketing budget, and performance-channel investment of tier-one competitors.

Why it matters to Lululemon

P.E Nation is the fastest relative mover in AU search: +100% in 3 months (1.5% to 3.0%). From a tiny base, but the acceleration is notable for a premium AU-native brand. This is the most likely brand to chip into Lululemon's premium AU-native positioning. A conquesting opportunity exists now, before P.E Nation builds further momentum.
