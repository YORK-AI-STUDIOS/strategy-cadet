The Upside A premium AU luxury athleisure label. Net-A-Porter, FWRD, and David Jones stockist. Flat SoS signals a brand at or near its organic ceiling.

Private (AU-founded, est. 2013)
Verified
The brand

Premium positioning, limited paid voice. The luxury niche is defensible but not growing.

The Upside is a premium Australian activewear brand (est. 2013, founder Jodhi Meares) occupying the luxury athleisure tier. Print-forward aesthetic, Italian and Australian-milled technical fabrics, and international stockists including Net-A-Porter and FWRD. It competes directly with Lululemon's premium positioning.

Media footprint is concentrated in premium editorial and select influencer partnerships, with no evidence of significant paid media investment at scale in AU. At 1.1% SoS with zero movement in either window, the brand appears to have reached its organic search ceiling.

Why it matters to Lululemon

The Upside is not currently a threat: flat at 1.1% SoS despite being well-resourced and premiumly positioned. It is possibly over-distributed in wholesale with weak direct search intent. In a market moving toward value-seeking behaviour, its premium positioning and limited voice leaves it exposed to share erosion from below, though its niche remains defensible while maintained. Low defensive investment warranted from Lululemon.
