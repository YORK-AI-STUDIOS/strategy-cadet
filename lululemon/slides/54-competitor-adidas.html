The brand

A brand in documented global revival, absorbing Nike's lost AU search share.

Adidas holds 10% AU market share (2025) and is in a documented global brand revival, having increased marketing spend by 12% in its most recent reporting period with further increases planned for 2025. Its ad performance data shows AU-relevant product lines (women's sport, athleisure) outperforming Nike equivalents on creative effectiveness.

Adidas runs 60+ active Meta ads (image-dominant, polished with lifestyle retro positioning), competing across performance and lifestyle segments through high distribution density in department stores, sports retailers, and DTC channels. Instagram followers: 29.5M global.

Why it matters to Lululemon

The Nike-to-Adidas migration is the dominant structural trend in AU athleisure search right now. Lululemon is not capturing Nike's shed share: Adidas is. If consumers are rotating from Nike to Adidas (sport-performance and lifestyle crossover) rather than to Lululemon (premium athleisure), the category conversation is moving away from Lululemon's core lane.
