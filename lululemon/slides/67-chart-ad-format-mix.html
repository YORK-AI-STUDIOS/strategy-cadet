What this means
Lululemon runs 6x more active Meta ads than Nike or Adidas. Evergreen-stacking, not campaign bursts.

The competitive moat on Meta is sheer creative volume + always-on architecture. Long-running ads (Align Pants 1,612 days; ABC Pant 744 days) confirm performing creative is never retired. The brand-building work (YET, Together We Grow, TV) runs in entirely separate channels.
