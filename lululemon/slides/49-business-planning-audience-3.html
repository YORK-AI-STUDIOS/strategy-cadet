Plan read

For this cohort the plan is a loyalty-seeding engine operating below full-price entry. The SweatLife Hub, pre-loved Loop entry, student discount, and campus collective all serve the same function: habituate brand relationships before full earning power is established. Every concept is delivered in the channels this cohort actually lives in: TikTok, creators, campus, and community. Metrics are engagement and conversion, not reach and awareness.
