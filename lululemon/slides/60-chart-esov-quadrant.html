What this means
Lululemon is the only premium brand in the expansion quadrant. The competitive heat is from Adidas, not from AU-natives.

Lululemon's mindshare exceeds physical footprint, putting it in expansion despite under 50 stores. Adidas runs a parallel expansion play with paid investment. Nike defends. Lorna Jane defends with community.
