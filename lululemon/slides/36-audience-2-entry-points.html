Read across
Social proof and commitment mechanics are the dominant doorways.

This cohort enters via social validation (run club peers, boardroom signals) and commitment triggers (new training block, milestone reward). The gear purchase functions as a commitment device, not a reward for existing behaviour. Once through the door, loyalty is high: ABC pants that fit are not replaced without a reason.
