Read across
Five of seven triggers are life-stage or emotional. The purchase precedes the commitment.

This audience buys Lululemon before consistently attending Pilates, before committing to the gym routine, before the habit is formed. Social proof (TikTok, peer challenges) opens the category. Emotion converts. Life-stage moments (first job, new semester) create permission to spend. Acquiring this cohort at first purchase is a long-term lifetime value investment.
