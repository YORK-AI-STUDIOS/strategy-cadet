Read across
Life-stage and emotional triggers dominate; the studio ritual is the recurring doorway.

Occasion-led moments (ritual lock-in, seasonal refresh) are the highest-frequency triggers. Emotion-led moments (stress reset, gift to self) drive higher basket value. Social proof at the studio converts consideration into first purchase. Always-on community presence rewards this audience more than campaign bursts.
