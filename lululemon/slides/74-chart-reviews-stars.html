What this means
The brand's physical retail is the customer experience asset. The digital channel is the liability. The two have diverged enough to be different brands in customer perception.

Flagship city stores read mixed-positive (knowledgeable staff, sizing-savvy). Online store reads as a returns-friction crisis. Factory Outlets specifically cited as "the worst." The 1.4★ online figure is the AU-specific signal that needs intervention before any creative work lands.

  • Three complaint themes: Returns rigidity (60%+), quality decline (40-50%), CS deflection to AI (35-40%).
  • r/lululemon ~700K members; sentiment broadly negative on quality globally.
  • Lululemon's repair programme excludes pilling - explicit policy on the dominant complaint pattern.
