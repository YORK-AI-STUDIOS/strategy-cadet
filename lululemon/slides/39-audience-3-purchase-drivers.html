What this means
The single biggest gap is TikTok / IG discovery delivery vs importance. Lululemon under-invests on the channel where this cohort lives.

Brand image at maximum importance with maximum delivery is the moat. The “hot girl walk” and Align legging viral cycles already convert. But Lululemon TikTok (1.4M followers) trails Adidas (11.2M) and Nike (7M+) heavily: organic UGC is huge, brand-owned reach lags.

  • Gen Z drives 41% of annual wellness spend despite being a third of adult population (McKinsey 2025).
  • 29% of Australians engage in “doom spending”; athleisure is a key beneficiary category (CBRE / UTS).
  • BNPL, gifting and sale-timing normalise premium purchases on lower incomes (AUD 25k to 65k).
